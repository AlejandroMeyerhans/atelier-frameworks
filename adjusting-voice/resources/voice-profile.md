## Dimensions to Cover

### Layer 1 — Surface (How It Sounds)
1. **Voice Register** — Formal/informal, direct/indirect, your relationship to the reader
2. **Sentence Architecture** — Sentence length, rhythm, punctuation habits, paragraph style
3. **Word Selection** — Vocabulary level, jargon usage, metaphor domains, recurring phrases

### Layer 2 — Structure (How It's Built)
4. **Content Architecture** — Opening patterns, closing patterns, transitions, section organization
5. **Rhetorical Machinery** — Storytelling style, framing techniques, authority signals, emotional levers
6. **Formatting Fingerprint** — Paragraph length, white space, header style, visual rhythm

### Layer 3 — Depth (How You Think)
7. **Cross-Medium Invariance** — How your voice stays consistent (or adapts) across writing, speaking, social
8. **Negative Space** — What you NEVER do (often the strongest calibration signal)
9. **Cognitive Sequencing** — How you build arguments: evidence-first or conclusion-first, concrete→abstract or vice versa
10. **Emotional Signature** — Your emotional arc: opening emotion, peak placement, aftertaste
11. **Signature Moves** — The 3-5 patterns that are unmistakably you
12. **Trust Architecture** — How you establish credibility
13. **Cultural & Linguistic Texture** — Cultural references, bilingual patterns, regional idioms
14. **Humor & Wit Architecture** — How you use (or avoid) humor: frequency, type, placement, boundaries
